About Us

Electronics is a branch of physics and engineering focused on the study and application of electrical circuits involving active electrical components such as transistors, diodes, and integrated circuits. It plays a crucial role in various technological advancements, powering devices like smartphones, computers, and medical equipment.

The Electronics Department is dedicated to advancing knowledge and innovation in the field of electronics. Our mission is to provide top-quality education and research opportunities, preparing students to excel in the rapidly evolving electronics industry. We offer a comprehensive curriculum that covers both theoretical foundations and practical applications, supported by state-of-the-art laboratories and experienced faculty.
